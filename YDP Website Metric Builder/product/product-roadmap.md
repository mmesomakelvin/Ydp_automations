# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Overview Dashboard
Live program metrics from the matches sheet: total mentees, mentors, and matches, average pair score, track distribution, and status/risk breakdowns.

### 2. Mentee Lookup
A mentee enters their ID and sees all their matched mentors, ranked by pair score, with track and match status.

### 3. Mentor Lookup
A mentor enters their ID and sees the mentees matched to them, with pair score, track, and status.

### 4. Match Directory
A searchable, filterable table of every match — filter by track, status, risk, and score.
