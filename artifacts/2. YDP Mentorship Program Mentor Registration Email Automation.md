## Planned Automation

The mentor automation will follow the same control pattern as the mentee automation:

1. Automatically send a registration email for future form submissions.
2. Manually send an update email to mentors who registered before installation.
3. Add status and sent-at columns to prevent accidental duplicate emails.
4. Provide test, preview, selected-row, bulk-send, and force-resend controls from a custom Sheet menu.
