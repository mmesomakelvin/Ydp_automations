# 1. YDP Mentorship Program Mentee Registration Email Automation

## Purpose

Define the Apps Script automation for emails sent to YDP Mentorship Program mentee applicants after registration, including emails for applicants who have already registered.

## Automation Scope

- Send a confirmation email after a mentee applicant registers.
- Send a separate email to mentee applicants who have already registered, if needed.
- Define the email triggers, recipient rules, message content, and required applicant data.

## Questions To Answer

1. What should mentee applicants who already registered receive?
2. Should the email include a timeline for selection, onboarding, or next steps?
3. Should the email include links, calendar details, community links, or attachments?
4. What reply-to email address should be used?
5. Should the automation prevent duplicate emails if the same person submits the form more than once?

## Confirmed Decisions

- Audience label: mentee.
- New registration message: confirm their registration.
- Next step: wait for review and check email later for mentor matching details.
- Commitment message: mentees must be ready to commit to the program.
- Sender name: YDP Mentorship Team.
- Start date/onboarding date: July 10, 2026.
- Tone: tell them we are happy to have them as a mentee.
- Already registered mentees should receive a separate follow-up/update email, not the same new-registration email.

## Open Decisions

1. Reply-to email address.
2. Whether to include any links in the confirmation email.
3. Whether duplicate form submissions should receive another email or be skipped.

## Draft Email Types

### Mentee New Registration Email

Status: Approved.

Subject: Your YDP Mentorship Program registration has been received

Hi {{First Name}},

## Apps Script Notes

- Use `Email Address`, `First Name`, and `Last Name` as the core personalization fields.
- Add an email status column before sending production emails, such as `Mentee Registration Email Status`.
- Add a timestamp column for auditability, such as `Mentee Registration Email Sent At`.
- Use the form submit trigger for new registrations.
- Use a manual/send-batch function for already registered mentee applicants.
- Email sender display name should be `YDP Mentorship Team`.

## Control Design

The Apps Script should support two sending flows:

1. Automatic sending for new mentee registrations after the automation is installed.
2. Manual sending for mentees who already registered before the automation was installed.

Recommended Google Sheet controls:

- Add a custom menu named `YDP Automation`.
- Menu options:
  - `Send test mentee email`
  - `Send mentee email to selected row`
  - `Send mentee email to all unsent rows`
  - `Send already registered mentee update`
  - `Preview selected row email`
  - `Setup email tracking columns`

Recommended tracking columns:

| Column name | Purpose |
| --- | --- |
| Mentee Registration Email Status | Tracks whether the normal registration email was sent. |
| Mentee Registration Email Sent At | Stores when the normal registration email was sent. |
| Already Registered Email Status | Tracks whether the existing-registrant email was sent. |
| Already Registered Email Sent At | Stores when the existing-registrant email was sent. |
| Email Last Error | Stores the latest send error for troubleshooting. |

This avoids duplicate emails and lets the team control bulk sends from the sheet.
