## Planned Automation

The mentor automation will follow the same control pattern as the mentee automation:

1. Automatically send a registration email for future form submissions.
2. Manually send an update email to mentors who registered before installation.
3. Add status and sent-at columns to prevent accidental duplicate emails.
4. Provide test, preview, selected-row, bulk-send, and force-resend controls from a custom Sheet menu.

## Confirmed Decisions

- New mentor registrations receive an automatic confirmation email.
- Mentors who registered before installation receive a separate update email.
- The onboarding/start date is July 10, 2026.
- Mentors should watch their email for mentee-matching details and be ready to commit.
- Sender name: `YDP Mentorship Team`.

## Mentor New Registration Email

Subject: `Your YDP Mentorship Program registration has been received`

```text
Hi {{First Name}},

Thank you for registering for the YDP Mentorship Program.

We are happy to have you as a mentor.

We have received your registration and our team will review your submission. Please keep an eye on your email, as we will get back to you soon with details about your mentee matching.

The program starts with onboarding on July 10, 2026. As we move into the matching stage, we ask that you remain ready to commit fully to the mentorship program and make the most of the opportunity when matched.

Warm regards,
YDP Mentorship Team
```
